Breakfast
Bolognese Sauce
beefpastamealprep
🍝
Ingredients
11 items
Steps
7 steps
Ingredients
  • 500g mince beef
  • 1 onion diced
  • 4 garlic cloves minced
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 1 carrot
  • Basil bunch
  • Parmesan rind
  • 1/2 glass of red wine
  • 1/2 cube beef stock
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 jar passata
Video
Method
  • 01Dice all vegetables and set aside. Saute onion and garlic until fragrant. Add celery and carrots.
  • 02Once everything has slightly softened, add the mince beef and start breaking it apart.
  • 03When meat starts browning, add the diced tomatoes and mix well.
  • 04Add jar of passata and fill half the jar with water, shake it to catch left over sauce, then add to the pot.
  • 05Crumble beef cube, add half the basil, parmesan rind, wine (if using) and mix well.
  • 06Leave uncovered and cook for a minimum of of 2 hours. Keep checking the sauce in case it needs more water to continue cooking.
  • 07Once cooked, add a teaspoon of sugar, pinch of bicarb soda (if sour), the other half of the basil, and adjust salt & pepper if needed.
